About Us

E-Cell, LNMIIT is a group of passionate and exuberant students at LNMIIT, Jaipur working on promoting the Startup culture and a spirit of Entrepreneurship among the youngsters. Being one of the most active E-Cell’s in Jaipur, we engage in events related to Entrepreneurship on a frequent basis in and outside Jaipur.



Not just this, we ourselves organize a wide variety of events and competitions for the brain storming of budding entrepreneurs.  We actively participated and won Entrepreneurship related events. Two of our most charismatic members won the Rajasthan Startup Fest when others volunteered in the same which marked the beginning of a great academic year ahead.  We ourselves organized appathon with Think Space Jaipur to provide a great learning experience to our members as well as students of other colleges. We also organized two major events in our annual Techno-Management Fest- PLINTH which marked over 400 students gathering and participating in the event - "Startup Intern Fair". We also managed to get a highly qualified judging panel for our live crowd funding cum B-Plan competition - "Negotio". To mark our presence at the end of the academic year we organized "Sell with E-Cell" - Bazaar by Entrepreneur to engage all the students of the college and bring out the hidden entrepreneur in them.
